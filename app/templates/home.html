About ivrit.ai

ivrit.ai is a non-profit effort aiming to make Hebrew a first-class citizen for AI technologies, by providing favorably-licensed datasets.

This Explore website allows you to search through the audio transcripts in the dataset.

All data is licensed under the ivrit.ai license and is also available in the Huggingface repository.

For support, help, ideas and contributions, please contact us at info@ivrit.ai
